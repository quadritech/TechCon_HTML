History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts in Silicon Valley. What started as a 200-person event has grown into one of the world's premier technology conferences, attracting over 10,000 attendees annually from across the globe. Over the years, TechCon has been at the forefront of showcasing breakthrough technologies, from the early days of mobile computing to today's AI revolution.

Mission

Our mission is to accelerate technological innovation by bringing together the brightest minds in the industry. TechCon serves as a catalyst for breakthrough ideas, fostering collaboration between startups, established corporations, researchers, and investors. We believe that by sharing knowledge and connecting visionaries, we can solve the world's most pressing challenges through technology.

Past Speakers

Dr. Sarah Chen

Leading AI researcher and former Director of Machine Learning at Google. Dr. Chen's groundbreaking work in neural networks has shaped modern artificial intelligence. Her TechCon 2023 keynote on "The Future of Human-AI Collaboration" was attended by over 5,000 participants.

Marcus Rodriguez

Blockchain pioneer and founder of CryptoSecure Solutions. Marcus has been instrumental in developing secure cryptocurrency protocols and spoke at TechCon 2022 about "Decentralized Finance: Building Trust in Digital Economies."

Prof. Elena Volkov

Quantum computing expert from MIT and lead researcher on quantum supremacy projects. Professor Volkov's presentation on "Quantum Computing: Beyond the Hype" at TechCon 2021 provided insights into practical applications of quantum technology.
